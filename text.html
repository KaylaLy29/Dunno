How to Start a Fanfiction

Hello! I am a certified degenerate and it is my aim to encourage others to be one too! This website will show you all the nooks and crannies of how to write a successfull, Twitter fueling fanfiction! The girls that get it, get it.

Things You'll Need

  • A computer/phone
  • Pen and paper
  • Serious resove
  • Multiple mental illnesses
  • Little time to actually commit to the story so that when your story blows up you disappear off the face of the earth and never finish the story

Step One: An idea

After gathering all of your tools you're gonna want to decide the 5 W's and the H of your story. Who do you want to be in the story? What is the storyline? Why are you doing this instead of your homework that was due three days ago? Where is this story taking place? How do you want this story to go? All of these questions are imperative to the planning and organization of your story.

  1. Who?
  2. What?
  3. When?
  4. Where?
  5. Why?
Take a pen and paper and jot down any and all ideas that you have and where you may want your story to go.
Note: Remember, the success of your story is all in the community you choose to represent with this story. Whether that's MHA, Harry Potter, BTS, Harry Styles -- anything and everything -- remember the audience you're catering to.

Step Two: A Platform

Also imperative to your success, a place to showcase your writing is a very very very important aspect of your story. Wattpad and A03 are the most well known and popular, but that also calls for a lot of competition. When choosing your platform(s) make sure it fits best right for you.
